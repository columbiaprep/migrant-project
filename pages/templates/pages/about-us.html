Our Mission

At MiCenterNYC, we are dedicated to easing the journey of migrants in New York City by providing a comprehensive, accessible, and community-driven online platform. Our mission is to empower migrants by connecting them with vital resources, support networks, and volunteering opportunities, ensuring that they find the assistance they need with ease. Through a user-friendly interface, multilingual support, and collaborative partnerships, we strive to foster a sense of belonging and empowerment within the migrant community while promoting unity and understanding across all backgrounds.

MiCoreValues
Inclusion
Fairness

Inclusion is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that every migrant, regardless of background or circumstance, deserves a place where they can feel welcomed and supported. Our platform is a space where diversity is celebrated, and everyone's voice is valued.

Fairness guides our actions. We are committed to providing equal opportunities and resources for all migrants, ensuring that no one is left behind. Whether it's access to information, services, or opportunities, we strive for fairness in every interaction.

Integrity
Security

Integrity is our foundation. We uphold the highest ethical standards, fostering trust and transparency within our community. From the information we share to the partnerships we form, integrity guides our decisions every step of the way.

Security is non-negotiable. We understand the importance of safety and privacy for migrants navigating new territories. Our platform is designed with robust security measures to protect personal information and ensure a secure environment for all.
